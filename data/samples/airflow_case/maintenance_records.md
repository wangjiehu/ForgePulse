# Maintenance Records

## Record M-2026-057

Line: cell coating line B

Symptom: fan frequency fluctuation caused local dryer temperature instability.

Resolution: cleaned airflow filter and replaced fan inverter cooling module.

Downtime: 38 minutes.

Lessons learned: fan frequency drift can amplify local temperature deviation and should be checked when temperature deviation is accompanied by airflow instability.

## Record M-2026-089

Line: cell coating line A

Symptom: after a power fluctuation, fan inverter output became intermittent. Zone 1 and zone 2 temperatures both drifted upward until the fan recovered.

Resolution: installed power line filter and replaced fan inverter capacitor bank.

Downtime: 55 minutes.

Lessons learned: power quality issues can trigger fan inverter instability. Consider adding UPS or power conditioning for the inverter drive.
