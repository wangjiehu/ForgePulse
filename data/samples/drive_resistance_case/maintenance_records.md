# Maintenance Records

## Record M-2026-103

Line: cell coating line A

Symptom: drive current gradually increased over a shift. Roller surface inspection revealed coating material buildup causing uneven grip and tension oscillation.

Resolution: cleaned all roller surfaces, replaced one worn drive roller bearing. Recalibrated tension controller after cleaning.

Downtime: 62 minutes.

Lessons learned: when DRV-410 and TEN-204 occur together, roller contamination is the most likely common cause. Regular roller cleaning schedule should be enforced after material changes.

## Record M-2026-078

Line: cell coating line C

Symptom: sudden drive current spike and tension loss. Found wrapped coating material on a guide roller.

Resolution: removed wrapped material, inspected roller surface, adjusted web path alignment.

Downtime: 28 minutes.

Lessons learned: web path alignment issues can accelerate material buildup on rollers. Periodic alignment checks are recommended.
