# Partial Dryer Alarm Procedure

## DRY-122 Initial Safety Check

1. Confirm the alarm timestamp and affected recipe.
2. Verify that the temperature historian channel is available and calibrated.
3. Do not replace components until temperature evidence is restored.

## Evidence Escalation

1. Collect controller output, heater current, and zone temperature.
2. Request manual inspection if the historian channel remains unavailable.
