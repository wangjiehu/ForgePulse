# Dryer and Airflow Evidence Verification

## DRY-122 Instrument Verification

1. Compare controller temperature with an independent calibrated sensor.
2. Check alarm timestamp alignment before replacing the heater or controller.
3. Record controller output and heater current.

## AIR-305 Fan Frequency Verification

1. Compare inverter command frequency with measured fan frequency.
2. Inspect the inverter cooling path and airflow filter.
3. Verify power quality before changing inverter parameters.
