# Maintenance Records

## Record M-2026-041

Line: cell coating line A

Symptom: zone 2 temperature overshoot after recipe switch, followed by unstable web tension.

Resolution: replaced aging heater relay and recalibrated zone 2 temperature sensor. Tension controller parameters were restored to baseline.

Downtime: 46 minutes.

Lessons learned: when DRY-122 and TEN-204 occur within 10 minutes, check temperature control loop before adjusting line speed.

## Record M-2026-057

Line: cell coating line B

Symptom: fan frequency fluctuation caused local dryer temperature instability.

Resolution: cleaned airflow filter and replaced fan inverter cooling module.

Downtime: 38 minutes.

Lessons learned: fan frequency drift can amplify local temperature deviation and should be checked when temperature deviation is accompanied by airflow instability.
